Welcome to the Digital Support Platform for Dementia Caregivers

This platform is designed to enhance caregiver well-being and patient outcomes by offering a centralized hub for managing caregiving tasks, accessing resources, and connecting with peers.

  • Caregiver Dashboard

    A centralized hub for managing caregiving tasks, accessing resources, and connecting with peers. The dashboard provides an overview of daily activities, reminders, and important updates, helping caregivers stay organized and informed.

  • Symptom Tracker

    Tools for monitoring and recording patient symptoms and health status. The symptom tracker allows caregivers to log changes in behavior, track the progression of dementia, and share information with healthcare providers for better care coordination.

  • Activity Planner

    Customizable plans for engaging patients in meaningful activities. The activity planner includes a variety of activities tailored to the interests and abilities of individuals with dementia, promoting cognitive stimulation and social interaction.

  • Medication Reminder

    Alerts and notifications for medication administration. The medication reminder ensures that caregivers administer medications on time, reducing the risk of missed doses and medication errors.

  • Resource Library

    Comprehensive educational materials and expert advice on dementia care. The resource library provides caregivers with access to articles, videos, and guides on topics such as symptom management, communication strategies, and self-care.

  • Community Forum

    Platforms for peer support and knowledge sharing. The community forum allows caregivers to connect with others facing similar challenges, exchange advice, and offer emotional support.
